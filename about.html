Our Story

GlowNature was born from a simple idea — that the best things for your skin already exist in nature. Founded by Theo Montgomery, a passionate believer in clean living and mindful self-care, this brand grew from a kitchen hobby into a mission to share natural healing with the world.

Each product is handcrafted with purpose, using only ethically sourced and organic ingredients. We’re committed to clean beauty that nourishes the skin without harming the environment. No harsh chemicals, no fake promises — just nature doing its thing.

What We Stand For

  • Transparency: You deserve to know what’s in your skincare.
  • Simplicity: We keep our ingredients minimal and meaningful.
  • Care: Every product is handmade with your well-being in mind.
  • Sustainability: Our packaging is eco-conscious and recyclable.
